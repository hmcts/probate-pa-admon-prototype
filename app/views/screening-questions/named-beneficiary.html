You could be a beneficiary who will get everything left by the person who died, not including items or gifts for specific people.

Some wills describe this as a ‘residuary legatee or devisee’ or someone who will receive the ‘residue of the estate’.

You could also be someone named to hold the estate ‘in trust’.
